DOCUMENTS REQUIRED FOR VISA APPLICATION

Please find the following details required document (as per updated border measures for entry to Singapore) to visit https://safetravel.ica.gov.sg/arriving/overview for further requirements necessary to enter Singapore for Singapore Visa processing purpose.

  • 01. Passport
    • a. Valid Passport (at least six (06) months validity from the date of arrival in Singapore).
  • 02. Photo
    • a. Recent Photo within 03 (Three) Months
    • b. Photo Size: 45mmX35mm Photo Guidelines
    • c. White Background (without any shadow)
    • d. MATT paper / Semi (finish) MATT Paper.
  • 03. Form 14A
    • a. Duly filled up (Completely) with Signature as per Passport.
    • b. Should be mention annual income equivalent SGD
    • c. Valid e-mail address
    • d. Valid Contact number
    • e. Address in Singapore / Hotel Details.
  • 04. Form V39A / LOI (Letter of Introduction from Singapore which will be verified through email).
  • 05. Bank Statements (Last Six Months).
  • 06. Bank Solvency Certificate (optional).
  • 07. Business Related documents (i.e Trade License, TIN etc.).
  • 08. JOB Related Documents
  • 09. Visiting Card
  • 10. Forwarding Letter / Visa asking Letter with Letterhead Pad
  • 11. Undertaking Letter
  • 12. Authorization Letter
  • 13. NID
  • 14. Others (if required).
  • 15. Travel Insurance (GENUINE) which covered @ 30,000.00 (not required for Fully Vaccinated applicants).

VISA APPLYING PROCESS

Assistance regarding Visa rules
  • A valid Email Address
  • A Valid Address
  • A valid (active) Contact Number

Pre-Applications checks
  • Please be very clear on your purpose of visiting the Republic of Singapore and be noted that we will assist for all of your visa applications without guidance on choosing your visa category accordingly. For further more and details information you may visit https://www.mfa.gov.sg or www.ica.gov.sg

Submission of Visa Application
  • Please read carefully the instructions and checklist for required documents before visiting the AVA (Authorized Visa Agent) office. For any kinds of Singapore Visa process related assistance you are requested to contact our visa department with Passport and related Form and documents.

  • The applicant / representatives are requested to address their application with the applicable documents as per their category to:

  • To
    The Second Secretary (Visa & Consular)
    High Commission of the Republic of Singapore
    Ventura Avenue, 5th Floor
    8B Gulshan Avenue,
    Dhaka 1212
    BANGLADESH.

  • If your Visa Application Form 14A, LOI (V39A) form, Undertaking, Authorized Letters with required related documents are not completed, you are requested to complete as per guideline and submit your application.

  • Each applicant must pay the applicable Visa Fee and Service Charges in cash mode only while submitting your visa application to our counter by collecting the acknowledgement receipt that must be submitted to our delivery counter at the time of passport collection.
